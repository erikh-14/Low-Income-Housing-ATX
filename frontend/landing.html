Affordable Housing ATX (AHATX)

Created By: Phillip Yang & Erik Hernandez

What is the AHATX?

It is Affordable housing search tool website. We show affordable rental properties based on your household income, household size and zip code where you want to live in the City of Austin.

Do I qualify for income-restricted housing?

Home seekers must meet specific household income requirements to qualify to rent or purchase an income-restricted affordable unit. Most funding and incentive programs in Austin require that a households gross annual income is no more than 80 percent of the median family income.

What features can I use?

  • Search Tool: Enter your current yearly household income and how many people reside in your household.
  • Favorites: You can save properties that you want to check later.
  • Recommendations: once you have at least one favorite, we are finding the housing you might like in different zip codes.

Is the housing list reliable and up to date ?

Yes, we got affordable housing data from austintexas.gov which is a reliable source. Web admin keeps updating housing lists when the property is updated from austintexas.gov

What about afforable hosuing that has a waitlist?

Currently, we do not provide information for the waiting list for each property. For more accurate information, you need to contact the property manager to get on the waitlist. You can find the property website and phone number from our website.
